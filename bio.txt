Paul Liang is an Assistant Professor at the MIT Media Lab and MIT EECS. His research advances the foundations of multisensory artificial
intelligence to enhance the human experience. He is a recipient of the MIT Sony Career Development Chair Professorship, Forbes 30 under 30 in Science,
CMU School of Computer Science Distinguished Dissertation Award, Siebel Scholars Award, Waibel Presidential Fellowship, Facebook PhD Fellowship,
Center for ML and Health Fellowship, Rising Stars in Data Science, and 4 best paper awards. Outside of research, he received the
Alan J. Perlis Graduate Student Teaching Award for developing new courses on multimodal machine learning.
